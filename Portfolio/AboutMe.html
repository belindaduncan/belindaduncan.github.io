Then

My journey has been one of dedication and adventure, marked by a significant chapter in the United States Marine Corps from 2003 to 2013. During this transformative decade, I served as an Electrical Equipment Repair Specialist, where I honed my skills in maintaining and repairing diesel power generators, a role that required both technical expertise and resilience. My service took me across the globe to countries like Japan and Korea, exposing me to diverse cultures and experiences. A pivotal part of my military career was my deployment to Iraq from 2008 to 2009, where I was part of the esteemed Lioness program. This specialized assignment not only underscored my commitment to my duties but also allowed me to contribute in a unique and impactful way, strengthening my resolve and enriching my perspective. This period of my life laid a strong foundation for my personal and professional growth, instilling in me values of discipline, precision, and adaptability that I carry to this day



Nowadays


Hello! I'm a dedicated Data Scientist working at a leading steel company in Kalama, Washington. My role involves analyzing complex data to drive innovation and efficiency in the steel industry. Outside of my professional life, I am a proud mother to a wonderful 8.5-year-old girl who inspires me every day. I also devote a significant part of my life to caring for my aging father, ensuring he enjoys comfort and happiness in his golden years. For my own well-being, I make time for regular workouts at the gym, which help me stay energized and balanced amidst my busy life. My journey is a blend of analytical thinking, nurturing love, and personal health, each aspect enriching the other.
